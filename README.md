# GeoMIP_for_CMIP7_analysis_code
Plotting and processing code to produce Figures in Visioni et al. (2026 - forthcoming); _The Geoengineering Model Intercomparison Project (GeoMIP) contribution to CMIP7_. 

**Contents**
1. temp_fig3.ipynb -- author: Cindy Wang (cw2239@cornell.edu) -- produces Figure 2, the evolution of global-mean temperature, interhemispheric temperature gradient, and equator-to-pole temperature gradient, under SSP2-4.5 G6-1.5K-SAI, ARISE-SAI-1.5 and G6-1.5K-HiLLA. Notebook runs on Derecho HPC.
2. G6-1.5K-SAI_HiLLA_example.iynb -- author: Alistair Duffey (alistair@reflective.org) -- produces Figure 4, an overview of SO2 injection magnitudes, temperature evolution and zonal temperature responses under the G6-1.5K-SAI and -HiLLA scenarios. Notebook runs on the [Reflective Cloud Hub](https://reflectivecloud.github.io/Book/intro.html). Over 40 TB of Data from G6-1.5K- simulations are freely available on the Reflective Cloud Hub. 

See [Lee et al. (preprint)](https://doi.org/10.5194/egusphere-2025-5742) for an overview of the G6-1.5K-SAI simulations, [Hirasawa et al. (preprint)](https://doi.org/10.5194/egusphere-2025-4810) for the G6-1.5K-MCB experiment, and [Visioni et al. (2024)](https://journals.ametsoc.org/view/journals/bams/105/12/BAMS-D-24-0274.1.xml) for a description of the protocol. The G6-1.5K-HiLLA protocol is discussed in [Visioni et al., (2025)](https://doi.org/10.1175/BAMS-D-25-0191.1) and testbed HiLLA simulation results are presented in [Duffey et al. (preprint)](https://doi.org/10.5194/egusphere-2025-5356).
